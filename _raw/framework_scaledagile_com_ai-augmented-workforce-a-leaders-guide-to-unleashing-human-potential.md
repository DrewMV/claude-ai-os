# AI Augmented Workforce: A Leader's Guide to Unleashing Human Potential

**Definition:** An AI-augmented workforce exists when employees use organizationally approved commercial or custom-built AI tools to optimize their work.

## What is an AI Augmented Workforce?

An AI-augmented workforce describes an environment where employees use AI to reduce repetitive tasks, improve quality, drive innovation, increase productivity, and complete tasks faster. AI works alongside humans, enhancing their abilities instead of replacing them so people can focus on important activities that require creativity, judgment, and teamwork. These benefits are unlocked when employees are given the authority, technical resources, and training to leverage AI to innovate and problem-solve.

AI can increase the productivity of individuals performing SAFe roles significantly. Scrum Master/Team Coaches can use AI to provide recommendations for improving team processes. Product management can use AI to accelerate market and customer research. Portfolio leaders can use AI to ideate well-formed strategic themes that help translate enterprise strategy to execution.

The outcome of supporting employee use of AI to augment their work is a more flexible and empowered workforce, where employees use their strengths while benefiting from AI's ability to work quickly, efficiently, and with better insights.

## What are the benefits of an AI augmented workforce?

AI is changing how employees work by performing tedious tasks like data entry and routine reporting. This allows employees to focus on more interesting and complex work, leading to personal growth and job satisfaction.

Research shows tangible benefits of using AI to support common business processes including improved productivity, quality, and decision-making speed.

AI affects productivity in many ways:
- By automating simple tasks, providing helpful insights, and speeding up decision-making
- AI lets individuals focus on work that matters and use their skills more effectively
- On the team level, AI tools improve collaboration by summarizing important meeting points, tracking tasks, and providing real-time insights into team performance

## How can AI augment key roles in SAFe?

Large Language Models (LLMs) such as SAFe CoPilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others enable users to access the world's knowledge through well-formed prompts to answer questions, organize information, analyze data, and create content.

Key SAFe roles and their AI use cases include:

## Five steps organizations can take to develop an AI Augmented Workforce

1. **Identify AI Tools and Use Cases**: Look for AI tools that meet specific needs and focus on options that enhance productivity and align with goals.
2. **Ensure Responsible AI Use**: Set Responsible AI policies to ensure AI tools respect data privacy, reduce bias, and maintain ethical standards.
3. **Measure and Monitor AI Impact**: Set up success metrics to assess how AI tools are improving productivity, quality, and innovation.
4. **Invest in Upskilling**: Provide employees with training to use AI tools, including both technical skills and strategic thinking.
5. **Foster a Culture of Innovation**: Encourage employees to try new things with AI and find solutions to problems.

## Pitfalls to Avoid with an AI Augmented Workforce

1. **Burnout due to unrealistic expectations combined with insufficient training and support.**
2. **Insufficient Responsible AI guidelines and capabilities.** Employees adopting AI tools without proper authorization or oversight pose significant data security risks.
3. **Overreliance on AI output.** An important principle of leveraging AI is the 'human in the loop' principle. AI provides the best value when it helps humans instead of replacing them.
