The SAFe Big Picture includes the following disciplines:

- **Lean Portfolio Management**

## Visualizing Lean Portfolio Management

### Solving Lean Portfolio Management Business Problems

Key elements: Enterprise | Portfolio Leadership | Portfolio Strategy | Epic Owners | Enterprise Architect | Epic | Portfolio Kanban | Value Management Office | KPIs | Products and Solutions | Portfolio Flow | Enabler | Coordination | Value Streams | Business Owners | Value Stream Funding | Guardrails | Lean Governance | Measure and Grow

The following Competencies address the most common business problems related to Lean Portfolio Management.

**[Organizing Portfolios](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/organizing-portfolios-competency/)**

Business Problem: We struggle to execute our desired strategy across the existing portfolio structures.

**[Lean-Agile Procurement](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/lean-agile-procurement-competency/)** [Community Contribution]

Business Problem: Our partner selection processes are slow, siloed, and misaligned with evolving business needs.

**[Validating Investment Opportunities](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/validating-investment-opportunities-competency/)**

Business Problem: We often make investment decisions that lead to unrealized value and wasted effort.

**[Enabling Agility with Enterprise Architecture](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/enabling-agility-with-enterprise-architecture-competency)**

Business Problem: Our inconsistent technology and design choices create duplicated technical efforts, poor user experiences, and increased costs.

**[Managing a Balanced Portfolio](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/managing-a-balanced-portfolio-competency)**

Business Problem: We struggle to balance immediate needs with strategic initiatives, leading to inefficient delivery of the most critical portfolio work.

**[Transitioning to Value Stream Funding](https://framework.scaledagile.com/lean-portfolio-management-discipline/transitioning-to-value-stream-funding-competency/)**

Business Problem: Our traditional approach to funding prevents us from being responsive to opportunities.

The following Competencies address the most common business problems related to Team and Technical Agility.

**[Creating Great Agile Teams](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/creating-great-agile-teams-competency/)**

Business Problem: Our teams are applying agile team practices, but we are slow to deliver and have low employee engagement.

**[Developing Quality Software](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/developing-quality-software-competency/)**

Business Problem: We have technical software delivery bottlenecks and frequent post-release defects with high maintenance costs.

**[Launching Agile Business Teams](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/launching-agile-business-teams-and-trains-competency/)**

Business Problem: We are not adapting to changing customer and market needs across our operational departments.

**[Continuously Delivering Value](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/continuously-delivering-value-competency/)**

Business Problem: We have no clear visibility into our end-to-end delivery processes that are slow, error-prone, and inefficient.

**[Marketing with Agility](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/marketing-with-agility-competency/)**

Business Problem: Our marketing efforts struggle to keep pace with emerging requirements and changing priorities, leading to less customer impact.

**[Implementing the Architectural Runway](https://framework.scaledagile.com/team-and-technical-agility-discipline/implementing-the-architectural-runway-competency)**

Business Problem: Our architecture is defined up front, often with minimal collaboration, making it difficult to respond to emerging requirements.

## Visualizing Product Development Flow

### Solving Product Development Flow Business Problems

Key elements: Product Innovation | Flow | Release on Demand | Business Value | Customer Centricity | Products and Solutions | Vision | Roadmap | Continuous Delivery Pipeline | Product Marketing | Feedback

The following Competencies address the most common business problems related to Product Development Flow.

**[Organizing Teams and ARTs Around Value](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/organizing-teams-and-arts-competency)**

Business Problem: We struggle to organize our workforce effectively around our products to optimize speed and agility.

**[Measuring Product Performance](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/measuring-product-performance-competency/)**

Business Problem: Inconsistent or missing metrics inhibit our ability to take data-driven decisions and effectively shape our product strategy.

**[Accelerating Product Flow](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/accelerating-product-flow)**

Business Problem: We are losing our competitive edge due to delays in delivering timely products and services that address customer needs.

**[Harnessing Customer Feedback](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/harnessing-customer-feedback-competency/)**

Business Problem: We do not capture and use customer feedback or share learnings across our organization, resulting in features no one wants.

**[Creating Responsive Roadmaps](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/creating-responsive-roadmaps-competency/)**

Business Problem: Our product and roadmaps are poorly prioritized, inflexible, and quickly become out of date, making it difficult to adapt to changes.

**[Integrating Product Design](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/integrating-product-design-competency)**

Business Problem: We have not integrated design in our Lean-Agile ways of working, resulting in poorly received products.

**[Scaling Agile Requirements](https://framework.scaledagile.com/product-development-flow-discipline/scaling-agile-requirements-competency)**

Business Problem: We struggle to create clearly defined Agile requirements that connect strategy and execution, resulting in rework and delays.

The following Competencies address the most common business problems related to Large Solution Integration and Delivery.

**[Coordinating Large Solution Delivery](https://framework.scaledagile.com/large-solution-integration-and-delivery-discipline/coordinating-large-solution-delivery-competency/)**

Business Problem: We struggle to coordinate and deliver value across multiple teams, specialists, and suppliers to deliver integrated products and systems.
