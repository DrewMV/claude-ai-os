# ServiceNow Community — Simplify Compliance to NERC CIP

**URL**: https://www.servicenow.com/community/grc-blog/simplify-compliance-to-nerc-cip-with-servicenow/ba-p/2838673

## What It Covers

ServiceNow's official community blog post mapping platform capabilities to NERC CIP standards. Useful for understanding the vendor's intended compliance use case positioning.

## Key Claims

**CIP standards addressed:**
- **CIP-003**: Platform capabilities "tether assets to controls"
- **CIP-007**: Integrated Risk Management (IRM) + CMDB used to manage IT and OT assets
- **CIP-009**: Business Continuity Management handles backup/recovery with regular testing
- **CIP-010**: Manages "change and vulnerability management processes" with "evidentiary support to auditors"
- **CIP-013**: Third-party Risk Management ensures suppliers adhere to NERC CIP best practices

**Key language:**
- CMDB "manage[s] your IT and OT assets"
- Systems are "constantly changing" and require ongoing oversight
- Platform provides evidence that "auditors" can review
- "Advanced Security Protection with ServiceNow Vault" for BCSI in cloud environments

## Limitations

- Does not specify how CSDM structures OT assets specifically
- Does not reference Configuration Management Plans or CCB Charters
- Emphasizes that "Registered Entities retain full responsibility" for implementing and configuring controls within their ServiceNow instance
