## PI Objectives — CMDB-CSDM

| # | Objective | Business Value | Committed |
|---|-----------|---------------|-----------|

| 2 | Establish CI Data Certification Program — Define certification framework, pilot for Business Applications, establish repeatable certification cadence. "Extend to other areas per team capacity" is a stretch goal / PI3 objective — not a PI2 commitment. | 8 | Yes |
| 3 | Expand Service Mapping Foundation — Expand Service Mapping to cover 10 per month of business-critical platforms within PI2 (May 13–Aug 4). | 9 | Yes |
| 4 | Prepare CMDB for Regulatory and Security Integrations — Define 100% of the foundational data model and integration approach for NERC CIP and Qualys supplemental CI data; ensure alignment with compliance requirements and linked to insights. | 8 | Yes |
| 5 | Embed NowAssist AI Across CMDB and ITSM Workflows — Accelerate CMDB data quality and ITSM efficiency by embedding NowAssist AI capabilities into key workflow touchpoints. | 7 | Yes |

Each migrated workload's Azure CI automatically discovered, reconciled against VMware predecessor, updated with accurate ownership, service relationships, and NERC CIP compliance attributes. No loss of CMDB fidelity across migration boundary. | 10 | Yes |
