Service Delivery

Underlying physical or logical CIs that support and run the Service Instances. Managed by infrastructure and platform teams.

Application Hosting
Hosting infrastructure for the ServiceNow platform stack
Note: The Service Delivery System is part of the Service Delivery Domain — alongside Service Instances. It is not a separate domain. Both describe operational reality: Service Instances are what runs; the Service Delivery System is what they run on.

Business Application

Strategic concept — maps to budget lines, vendor contracts, and roadmap decisions in your portfolio.

Service Instance

Operational reality — the specific running deployment your teams release to and operate.

Service Delivery System

Infrastructure foundation — the CIs your platform and infra teams own that keep Service Instances alive.

Why it matters for SAFe PMs

Traceability from an infra incident (SDS) → affected environment (SI) → business investment at risk (BA) — surfaced at PI planning.
