# SAFe Ceremony Schedule — Iteration Template

Reusable cadence for a standard **2-week (10 working day)** SAFe iteration.
Iteration **starts Wednesday, ends Tuesday** (weekends excluded). All times **EST**.

## Daily Cadence

| Ceremony | When | Time (EST) | Duration | Attendees |
|----------|------|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Daily Stand-up (DSU)** | Every working day (Day 1–10) | **8:30 AM** | 15 min | Whole team |

## Iteration Schedule

| Day | Weekday | Ceremony | Time (EST) | Duration | Purpose |
|-----|---------|----------|------------|----------|---------|
| **Day 1** | Wed (Wk 1) | DSU + Iteration Planning (combined) | 8:30 AM | 1.5 hrs (8:30–10:00) | Quick sync, then commit to iteration goals & pull stories into the sprint |
| Day 2 | Thu | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 3 | Fri | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 4 | Mon (Wk 2) | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| **Day 4** | Mon | Backlog Refinement | 10:00 AM | 1 hr | Groom & estimate upcoming backlog |
| Day 5 | Tue | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 6 | Wed | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 7 | Thu | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| **Day 7** | Thu | Backlog Refinement | 10:00 AM | 1 hr | Groom & estimate upcoming backlog |
| Day 8 | Fri | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 9 | Mon (Wk 3) | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| Day 10 | Tue | DSU | 8:30 AM | 15 min | Daily sync |
| **Day 10** | Tue | Iteration Review (Demo) | 10:00 AM | 1 hr | Demo completed work; gather feedback |
| **Day 10** | Tue | Iteration Retrospective | 11:00 AM | 1 hr | Inspect & adapt the team's process |

## ART-Level Sync Touchpoints (recurring)

| Ceremony | When | Time (EST) | Duration | Purpose |
|----------|------|------------|----------|---------|
| Scrum of Scrums (SoS) | Every Monday — Day 4 & Day 9 | 3:00 PM | 30 min | Cross-team dependencies & impediments |
| PO Sync | Day 3 (Fri) & Day 8 (Fri) | 2:00 PM | 30 min | Scope, priorities, backlog alignment |

## Notes

- DSU is a fixed daily anchor at **8:30 AM EST** for the full 10-day iteration.
- Day 1 combines DSU + Iteration Planning into one 8:30–10:00 AM block; Review + Retro close Day 10.
- Refinement runs twice (Day 4, Day 7) to keep the next iteration's backlog ready.
- Offshore team works **4:00 AM–12:00 PM EST**; whole-team ceremonies are kept inside that
  overlap window. Refinement moved to 10:00 AM for offshore attendance. PO Sync remains at
  2:00 PM (PO/PM-led; offshore dev attendance not required). SoS is every Monday at 3:00 PM
  (SM/RTE-level; also outside the offshore window).
- Non-DSU times above are suggested defaults — adjust to the team's standing invites.
- This is an *iteration-level* template. PI-level events (PI Planning, System Demo,
  Inspect & Adapt) sit outside this cadence and recur once per Program Increment.
